Prof. As. Dr Arta Musaraj, is the Rector of University "Pavaresia" Vlore, Editor in Chief of Academicus International Scientific Journal, CEO of Entrepreneurship Training Center an initiative of SEED (Southeast Europe Enterprise Development), IFC and World Bank. She has been specialized in many prominent universities in the world, Master in Business Administration, Doctor in the Economic Sciences, and Professor for scientific research in the field of Business Communication and Administration. Since 1996, she has been Lecturer in Business Administration at several universities. She is the publisher of many educational texts, articles and scientific analysis, expert, consultant and certified trainer of IFC of World Bank, representative of the international known organizations in offering services for the business and administration in the Southeast Europe. Prof. As. Dr Arta Musaraj is a lecturer and co-partner of scientific research in international universities.

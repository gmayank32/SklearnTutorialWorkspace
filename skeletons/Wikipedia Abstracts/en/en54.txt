Club García Agreda are a Bolivian Proffesional Football Club based in Tarija. The team plays its home matches at Estadio IV Centenário. The club currently competes at Tarija Primera A. The club have a rivalvry with Ciclon. On October 2010 Garcia eliminated Ciclon in 2010 Copa Simon Bolivar and left them with only 1 point an no opportunity of reaching First Division

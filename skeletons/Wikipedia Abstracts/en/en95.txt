Hyperzionism is a fringe political philosophy which advocates the creation of a Greater Israel to include all the territories reputedly promised by God to Abraham and his descendants at various places in the Torah; mainly at Genesis 15:18-21, Deuteronomy 1:6-8, and Deuteronomy 11:24. These territories are variously interpreted as extending from the Mediterranean, or the Nile, or the Sinai, in the West; to the Euphrates, in the East. One such avowedly Hyperzionist group is Bead Artseinu .

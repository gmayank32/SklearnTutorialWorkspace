The Aspen Leaftier, Poplar Leafroller or Spotted Aspen Leafroller (Pseudosciaphila duplex) is a moth of the Tortricidae family. It is found from Quebec and New England to northern California and British Columbia. File:Pseudosciaphila duplex damage. jpg Damage File:Pseudosciaphila duplex egg. jpg Egg The wingspan is 20-25 mm. Adults are on wing from June to August. The larvae feed on the leaves of poplar, birch and willow.

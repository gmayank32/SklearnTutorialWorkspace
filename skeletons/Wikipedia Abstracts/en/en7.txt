The Master of the Rebel Angels is an anonymous master of the Sienese School, during the second quarter of the 14th century. He is only known by two panels of an ancient polyptych painted between 1340 and 1345. They can be seen at the. His name is derivated from one of these panels called The Fall of the Rebel Angels. The other panel depicts Saint Martin dividing his cloak with the beggar.

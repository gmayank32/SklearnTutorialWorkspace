David Naughton is a Scottish record producer and audio engineer. He has produced or co-produced many indie artists including Beth Rowley, Rachel Goswell, Mojave 3, Frankie Miller, and Nick Duffy and the Lilac Time. After building his career in London, Glasgow, Los Angeles and New York, he moved to Tokyo, where he produced Japanese indie acts such as Satoro Ono and The New House. As an engineer, he has worked with several major global artists including Robbie Williams, Kylie Minogue, Belle and Sebastian, Annie Lennox, Suede and Simple Minds. He now works as a producer in Australia.
